About Pakistan Cricket Board

The Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) is the governing body responsible for the development, promotion and management of cricket in Pakistan across all levels — national, domestic and grassroots.

Our Mission

To nurture world-class cricketers, deliver outstanding competitions and grow cricket's reach across Pakistan through sustainable programmes and strong governance.

History & Milestones

  • 1948 — PCB established to govern cricket in Pakistan after independence.
  • 1992 — Pakistan won its first ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • 2009–present — Major investments in domestic structure and player pathways.
