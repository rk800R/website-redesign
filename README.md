 PCB Website Redesign

This repository contains the redesigned front-end for the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) official website. The redesign includes several enhancements, improved usability, and more visually engaging content to showcase Pakistan's cricket achievements, teams, and players. The website provides an updated and mobile-responsive experience, with content organized into various sections for easier navigation.

 Files Description:

 1. `pcb.html`

* **Purpose:** The homepage of the PCB website.
* **Features:**

  * Hero section highlighting the Pakistan Cricket Board's mission.
  * Key sections like "Latest News," "Upcoming Matches," and links to various cricket teams and tournaments.
  * A spotlight on the PCB Hall of Fame with legends of Pakistani cricket.
  * Quick access to the PCB's official social media profiles.

 2. `news.html`

* Purpose: A page dedicated to displaying the latest news about Pakistan cricket.
* Features:

  * A grid of articles featuring the most recent news, such as player interviews, team updates, and press releases.
  * Each article includes a featured image, a title, a short description, and a "Read More" link.

 3. `match_report.html`

Purpose: Display detailed match reports for recent cricket games.
  Features:

  * A list of recent match reports, including detailed scores and match summaries.
  * Information about the teams, players involved, and notable events in the match.
  * Interactive elements such as "Read Full Report" buttons for more details.

 4. `halloffame.html`

* Purpose: A tribute section for PCB’s Hall of Fame inductees.
* Features:

  * A search and filter option to find inductees based on name or era (Pre-2000, 2000s, 2010s+).
  * A grid of inducted players, coaches, and contributors to Pakistan cricket, showcasing their achievements and induction year.
  * A nomination form for users to propose new Hall of Fame candidates.

 5. `photos.html`

* Purpose: A photo gallery to showcase memorable moments from Pakistan’s cricket history.
* Features:

  * A gallery layout with high-quality images of players in action, celebrations, and iconic moments.
  * Image descriptions and event details for each photograph.

6. `players.html`

Purpose: A directory of current and past Pakistani cricketers.
Features:

  * Search functionality to filter players by name or region (Punjab, Sindh, etc.).
  * A grid of player cards displaying their photo, position, and region.
  * Interactive "View Profile" buttons that show detailed stats like ICC rankings, records, and player highlights.

 7. `podcast.html`

* **Purpose:** A page for PCB's official podcasts featuring interviews and discussions with players and experts.
* **Features:**

  * Embedded podcast audio players for episodes featuring prominent cricketers like Babar Azam and Shaheen Afridi.
  * A summary of each episode with information about the guest and the discussion.
